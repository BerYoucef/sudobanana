🧠 What is SudoBanana?

SudoBanana started as a solo side project — from someone who loves cybersecurity but kept forgetting everything written in notebooks 🫠 , Instead of letting the knowledge rot like forgotten fruit...
I turned it into a living wiki: casual in tone, serious in content, and weird enough to be memorable.
(You’ll get it soon — the banana might look funny, but it knows what it's doing.) 😉

🎯 Why this exists ?

📚 Document everything I learn.
🧪 Explain hard concepts in a simple, playful way.
🧠 Create a free, open reference for beginners, learners, and deep divers.
💡 Inspire others to make weird, meaningful projects too.
